# Our Lady of Forgiveness - Form Gallery

Sketchfab-backed Form Gallery route for **Donatello's Our Lady of Forgiveness (La Madonna del Perdono)** from Siena Cathedral.

Source:
- Sketchfab: https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/our-lady-of-forgiveness-donatello-siena-2a72b9f22da043eab0ff0988ac570ec0

The source description dates the sculpture to **1457-1459** and notes that the photogrammetry campaign was produced in collaboration with Opera del Duomo, Siena.
